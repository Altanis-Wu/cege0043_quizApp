Welcome to quizApp

The quizApp is designed for answering the quiz based on users’ location. Once launched the App it will track users automatically and the closest question will pop up. Users will be given the select list. When they submit their answers, the data will be uploaded onto the database and tell the users if the answer is correct or not. Some relevant records and their rankings are also accessible through this App.
To start the quiz, first of all, please launch the PhoneGap app. Next, enter the URL (e.g. http://developer.cege.ucl.ac.uk:31289/ and press “connect”. Then, you will be led to the home page of the App.
When you launch the App, the quiz layer will be loaded automatically. Meanwhile, the closet quiz point will pop up the questions. You can submit your question with the submit button.
NB.The App will track your movement and will update only when you are 10 meters away from your last location marker.
After the submission, you will get an alert to tell you that your answer is right or wrong.
What’s more, the matching marker will also change their colours. Red is wrong and green is correct.
Besides, you will get your records which excludes this latest submission.
Making use of the menu on right of the header bar, you can get your ranking and the help document.
Besides some advanced functions navigation, on the left-hand side bar, you can also get your id number (http number, 30xxx).
Pressing the top 5 scorers button, you can get the graph showing top players.
